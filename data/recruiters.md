# UAE/India Recruiter Outreach Tracker

**Purpose:** Aggressive 1-month job search. Direct LinkedIn outreach to recruitment consultants who place PMs in UAE/India. Faster than waiting for job boards — most good UAE roles get filled off-market through agencies.

**Target:** Contact 3-5 recruiters per day, 15-25 per week. Average reply rate 20-30%. Expect 4-7 active conversations by end of week 2.

## Playbook (30 min/day)

1. Open `data/recruiters.md` (this file)
2. Pick 3-5 recruiters tagged with matching specialty
3. LinkedIn search each: `"Senior Consultant" OR "Principal Consultant" {recruiter name} Dubai`
4. Filter to consultants who post PM/AI/digital roles (check their activity)
5. Send connection request + message (template below)
6. Log in the table: date contacted, consultant name, outcome

## Connection Request Template

```
Hi {Name}, I saw you recruit for {specialty} roles in UAE. I'm an AI Product Manager
with 10+ years building CCaaS/CRM/GenAI products (ex-Sprinklr, Salesforce, Kore.ai).
Relocating to Dubai in May. Would love to connect and see if any of your current
searches might be a fit. — Prernaa
```

## Follow-up Message (after connection accepts)

```
Thanks for connecting, {Name}. Quick snapshot:
- 10+ years AI Product Management (Sprinklr, Salesforce, Kore.ai)
- Domains: GenAI, CCaaS, Conversational AI, CRM, Martech, CX
- Target: Senior PM / AI PM / Principal PM / Product Owner
- Comp target: 45-60 LPA equivalent in AED (~275-350K AED)
- Locations: Dubai preferred, open to Bangalore / Singapore / Remote

Happy to share my CV if useful. What are you working on right now?
```

## Global Recruiter Safety Rules

1. **Never send your CV before connection + 2 message exchanges.** Recruiters who demand CV immediately are often low-quality.
2. **Never let a recruiter submit you without knowing the client name.** Get the company before they submit — otherwise duplicate submissions tank your candidacy.
3. **One recruiter per role.** If two recruiters pitch the same role, go with whichever contacted you first; tell the second you're already in process.
4. **Track every conversation here.** Update Contacted? + Outcome so you don't double-message or lose track.

## Notes

- UAE visa + relocation: mention willingness to start on visit visa with employer sponsoring work permit. Most UAE recruiters expect this.
- AED compensation conversion: 45-60 LPA INR ≈ 25-33K AED monthly / 300-400K AED annually. Add 15-20% for Dubai cost of living.
- Most UAE recruiters respond Sun-Thu (weekends are Fri-Sat). Don't ghost your own outreach by messaging on weekends.
